Math Tutor helping a student in stats

TRU Math Help Centre

The Math and Stats Help Centre is a free service for students that is staffed by mathematics and statistics faculty and upper-level students. It is open to students taking any TRU course that involves mathematics or statistics, but is most useful for UEPREP and first-year level courses. Students can work alone or together with other students in a relaxed, informal environment, with help readily available. For best results, students should bring their class notes, textbook and prior attempts.

Location

House Of Learning - third floor (HOL-304)

Timing

Monday - Friday: 9:30 a.m. - 5:30 p.m.

Saturday and Sunday: Closed

Including final Exam Period as well.

Writing Centre

The Writing Centre offers 45-minute face-to-face appointments with qualified graduate tutors during the fall 2018 and winter 2019 semesters. Students can book up to two appointments per week by logging in at tru.mywconline.com.

For TRU Open Learning students, writing centre offers free tutoring support, either in person or online. The Writing Centre has handouts, videos, tutorials, and web links that you can use to improve your skills in many areas.

TRU Campus Card

Campus Card Service

TRU can prepare campus card any time, but it can only be issued after your course registration is complete. TRU provides campus cards in two ways: By visiting the campus card booth in the bookstore, or by sending a selfie. All students need to have an activated UPASS in order for the card to be used on BC Transit and at the TCC. This must be activated every semester. New students may go the Bookstore for Campus Card production. Or, you can send in your selfie to selfie@tru.caand pick up your card at the TRUSU Members' Services Desk in the CAC

CAC - first floor (Left side inside Bookstore)

Security

TRU is committed to providing a safe and secure campus environment. campus security services are provided by Paladin Security on a 24-hour basis. They conduct foot patrols and respond to security and fire alarms and other campus emergencies.Security is responsible for opening and closing buildings on the campus. If you need out-of-hours access, security can provide this service but they do require advance authorisation and proof of identity.

Installing set up for remote access first time

TRU Remote Access

Remote access is for those who need access to their network drives from off campus, or those who are connected to the wireless network while on campus. TRU Remote Access web application provides a method for remote and secure access to data files from TRU network drives. It does not connect directly to the drives as you would when you are on campus, but does allow you to download/upload desired files to the local computer.

Location

Anwhere (should have an internet access on device)

Timing

24 Hours a day, 365 days a year
